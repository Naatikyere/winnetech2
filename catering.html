    WELCOME TO CATERING DEPARTMENT

    The Course is Catering and Hospitality industry. it is the art of providingfood drinks and sometimes accommodation to people. it is a vocation because trainees acquire skills they are able to earn a living and they can be self employed at the end of the training.

    The department has instructors with Higher National Diploma(HND) holders who are very sklled in the field of Hospitality management and we Take our time to guide student to be able to come out as the best versions of them selves and be able to make a living with the skills the learn.

    Mode of teaching is seventy percent(70%) Practicals and Thirty percent(30%) theory. Most students who are done with the training get to be self employed or they get employed in higher and reputable organisations and the become well to do in society.

    Attachment is very important so students are given the opportunity to go out on industral attachments where they get the chance to partically apply their vocational skill that they have learnt over the course of their stay to give them the feel of how its like to work in an external environment.
