    WELCOME TO WELDING DEPARTMENT

    My name is Ahoh Kwamena Eshan(Mr) the Head of DepartMENT (HOD) of welding department Winneba Technical Training Institude.I am an Advanced weilding craft practice holder and I manage this department with Mr Issac Naa Donkoh(National craftmanship certificate holder).

    Teaching bases for the welding department can be categoried into two section: THEORY and PRACTICALS, that is fourty Percent(40%) on THEORY which includes:

    • Trade Theory
    • Science and Calculation
    • Bench fitting
    • Black Smithing

    And We take sixty(60%) percent on trade Technology including:

    • Arc Weldind Practicals
    • Gas welding
    • Bench Fitting
    • Black Smitting Practice
    • Frabrication

    The course Range between two and half years for turtoring and six month for industrial training(O.J.T). The department take external contract from clients because it helps generate income for the department and also helps the trainees to get more knowledge on what they are being trained for.

    Trainees are allowed to take interest in other trade Courses such as Mechnical Engineering and Machineryand Plumbing and Gas fitting because most of these theory and practicals are offered in this department

    Welding is an essential component of many industries without welding many forms of metal works cannot be achieved examples Vehicle, Aircraft, Ships, Submarine and Sky buildings. After completing of course some trainees are able to established themselves, others also work in factories.
