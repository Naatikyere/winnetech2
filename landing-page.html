    Message From The Manager

    The Manager

    Mr Martin Ekow Young

    Winneba Technical Institude

    Hello and welcome to Winneba Technical Institude, Once called Winneba Vocational Training Institude which was chanced from the service of GES to Ghana TVET Service. We entreat that students who are privilaged to be enrolled under the Ghana TVET Service should know that the certificates that are awarded upon completion is higher than that of the vocational training institudes. So students are encouraged to study well since that puts them on a higher value in the job market.

    The school has been equipped with Ultra-modern tools and equipments to be used in making teaching and learning go as smoothly as possible and also enjoyable for maximum understanding of the various courses.

    The Institution is aimed at producing highly refined individuals with skill set who would be a part of the nation building to make Ghana great.

    We encourage you all to come to Winneba Technical Institude and learn a trade or sharpen your skills. Thank You!!!!

    Mission: The mission of his honorable institution is commmited to providing quality TVET Skills and industry led to persons of all ages and carreers,perparing them to be produtive citizens.

    Vision: We will prfovide the best system for employable.TVET Skills.

    core values

    In order to realize the vision and mission of the following core values,

    1. To improve quality of produt and service in the trained occupational areas.
    2. To ensure a more efficient transition of tranees from apprenticeship to master level.
    3. To increase number of small business start-up as more/tranees become more qualified.

    After one is done, which is the TVERT, the certificate they will be given are national proficiency one(1) and two(2) which is for all people who have undergone short to meddium term training. Also we have the national certificate one(1) and two(2) is also for students who have fully undergone the three years course, moreover the certificate can be used to further ones education at the university the university that is either HND or Fist Degree. When it comes to the perfomance of the previous exams,the pass rate has ranged between 95% and 100%. The school admit J.H.S and S.H.S leavers,self employed and paid workers who desire to acqure special skills and knowledge to improve upon their business and also unemployed people. The institution has the capacity to admit over 500 fresh students every acacdemic year. The student are admitde for both boarding and day. They also have a hostel facilities at the school that is managed by the school.

    Subjects

    Core

    1. English
    2. Mathematics
    3. Social Studies(E.S.T)
    4. I.C.T
    5. Science

    Electives

    1. Trade Science
    2. Trade Drawing
    3. Trade Practical
    4. Trade Drawing

    The Teaching bases in Winneba Technical instidue is in Two phases as Indicated, it is divided into Core and Elective section. The Core subjects are Term as Generics, since they are the subjects treated by all students in every department. Whiles The Electives are subjects treated only within the various departmental courses. Trade Practicals, Theory, Science and Drawing are all departmental subject based and it helps students to get more knowledge in all their various selected feels. So when you feel confused about the Various subjects please contact the Administrator for clarification.
